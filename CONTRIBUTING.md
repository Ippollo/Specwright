# Contributing

This is a personal toolkit — I built it for my own workflow and share it as inspiration.

## How to Use It

1. **Fork it** and make it your own.
2. **Adapt** the workflows, agents, and skills to fit your stack and style.
3. **Delete** anything you don't need.

## Pull Requests

I'm not actively accepting PRs, since this repo reflects my personal development methodology. But if you spot a bug or typo, feel free to open an issue and I'll take a look.

## Questions?

Open an issue if you're stuck or curious about how something works. I'm happy to help where I can.
